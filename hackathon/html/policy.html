Shipping Policy

We aim to deliver your orders as quickly and efficiently as possible. Please review our shipping policy below for details on delivery times, shipping methods, and costs.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $4.99 (Free on orders over $50)
Expedited Shipping 2-3 business days $9.99
Express Shipping 1-2 business days $14.99
International Shipping 10-21 business days Varies by destination

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on your location, shipping carrier, and any unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions or customs delays for international orders.

Order Processing

Once your order is placed, you will receive an order confirmation email. When your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will begin processing the next business day.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor your package's progress on the carrier's website.

Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to the following locations:

  • United States (all 50 states)
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Australia
  • European Union countries

For other international destinations, please contact our customer service team before placing your order to confirm availability and shipping costs.

Shipping Restrictions

Some items have special shipping requirements or restrictions:

  1. Fragile items: May require special packaging and handling fees.
  2. Hazardous materials: Cannot be shipped via standard methods.
  3. Oversized items: May incur additional shipping charges.
  4. Perishable goods: Have specific shipping requirements and may only be available for certain destinations.

International Shipping

For international orders, please note:

  • Delivery times may vary based on customs processing in your country
  • You are responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees
  • International orders may be subject to additional documentation requirements
  • Some products may not be available for international shipping due to legal restrictions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

If you need to change your shipping address, please contact our customer service team immediately at support@shopeasy.com or call (555) 123-4567. We can only change the shipping address if your order hasn't been processed for shipping yet.

What happens if my package is lost or damaged?

If your package is lost in transit or arrives damaged, please contact our customer service within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and may reship your order or issue a refund.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States. For international orders, free shipping is available on orders over $100.

Can I ship to a PO Box?

Yes, we can ship to PO Boxes via USPS. However, expedited shipping options are not available for PO Box addresses, and delivery times may be longer.

What if I'm not home when my package is delivered?

Most carriers will leave your package at your door or in a secure location. For high-value items, a signature may be required. If you're not available, the carrier will typically leave a notice with instructions for pickup or redelivery.
